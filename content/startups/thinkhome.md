# ThinkHome

A platform and service for refurbished notebook sales and IT solutions.

**Role**: Co-founder

## What We Do

Sourcing quality refurbished notebooks, providing B2B IT equipment, and delivering technical support. We aim to make high-quality hardware accessible and sustainable.

## Current Focus

- **B2B Partnerships**: Expanding our network of corporate clients.
- **Equipment Sourcing**: Ensuring consistent supply of premium devices.
- **Support Automation**: Streamlining customer service workflows.
